Mathematics Graduate Student Association

This is the website of the Mathematics Graduate Student Association (MGSA).

MGSA Purpose

The MGSA is the organization representing Mathematics and ACMS graduate students. The organization works to improve studying and working conditions for graduate students, encourages communication between students, faculty, and staff, and contributes to the student social life. Additionally, the MGSA treasurer coordinates the Graduate Student Seminar (GSS).

More information about the MGSA is available in its constitution.
